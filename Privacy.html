# Privacy Policy for Rozix View Last Updated: June 2026 Rozix View is an Android web browser developed by Rozix Systems. ## Information Collection Rozix View does not collect, store, or share personal information with Rozix Systems. ## Browsing Data Websites visited through Rozix View may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Rozix Systems does not receive or store browsing history on external servers. ## AI Ad Blocking Rozix View includes local AI-powered ad blocking and privacy protection features. These features operate locally on the device and do not transmit browsing data to Rozix Systems. ## Cookies Users can manage cookies through the browser settings. Cookies are handled by the websites visited and are not stored by Rozix Systems outside the browser environment. ## Third-Party Services Rozix View may access websites and online services selected by the user. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies. ## Data Security Rozix View is designed to operate locally on the user's device whenever possible. ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page. ## Contact Rozix Systems GitHub: https://github.com/sherifmousa707-droid
